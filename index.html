About me

Hey, I’m Eric, a designer who’s just as passionate about creating meaningful visual experiences as I am about staying active and leveling up in real life. My journey into design started with curiosity and has grown into a deep appreciation for how visuals, interaction, and storytelling shape the way we experience the world. Outside of design, you’ll probably find me at the gym, playing soccer, or diving into worlds like Cyberpunk 2077, Elden Ring, GTA V, or The Last of Us. I love how games blend art, sound, and interaction, the same creative principles that inspire my own approach to design. For me, design is about constant growth, pushing boundaries, learning from every challenge, and building experiences that connect with people.
